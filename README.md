# Customer Retention & Revenue Analysis

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior, retention patterns, and revenue concentration using SQL and Power BI.

The goal was to identify key revenue drivers and understand whether customer churn significantly impacts overall business performance.

---

## 🔧 Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- 93% of revenue is generated by repeat customers  
- Top 20% customers contribute ~75% of total revenue  
- One-time customers form ~34% of users but contribute less than 7% revenue  
- Customer churn is concentrated among low-value users  
- Retention is non-linear, indicating customer reactivation behavior
